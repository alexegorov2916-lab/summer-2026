Summer 2026: Dolomiti & Levanto Road Trip

✎ Prague → Dolomiti → Levanto → home again

🌎 Interesting Facts About the Countries We Passed

  • Czech Republic: Home to over 2,000 castles — one of the highest densities in the world.
  • Austria: More than 60% of the country is covered by the Alps.
  • Italy: Has more UNESCO World Heritage Sites than any other country.
  • Germany: Its Autobahn network is one of the oldest and fastest highway systems in Europe.

🚜 Family Road Trip Story

We packed our car in Prague with way too many snacks and absolutely no idea how many tunnels we were about to drive through. The Dolomiti greeted us with towering cliffs, Levanto welcomed us with warm waves, and on the way back we zig-zagged through Austria and Germany like seasoned road-trip pros. By the end, our car looked like it had survived a small natural disaster — but the memories were worth every crumb, every detour, and every "Are we there yet?" moment.
